Estd. Lockdown 2020

Born out of the necessity to spread cheer in the gloomy pandemic, we are all about handcrafted dark chocolate bars infused with the indigenous varieties of Himalayan nuts, fruits and condiments that evoke happiness.
With this happiness initiative, we focus on creating livelihood opportunities for local women whose households were disrupted. Training them in chocolate-making and incorporating traditional Himalayan techniques in our production, we are that homegrown, social enterprise who is proud of empowering lives.
